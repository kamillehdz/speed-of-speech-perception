Welcome to the experiment.


This experiment is designed to test your ability to comprehend speech at increasing video playback speeds.

When you are ready, press play on the video.

You can adjust the playback speed using the + and - or by using the slider beneath the video.

Use Reset to return the playback speed to 1.0x.

Stop the video as soon as you feel you can no longer understand the speech.
